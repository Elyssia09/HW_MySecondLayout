Zeterre Landscape Architecture
Redesign Zeterre Landscape Architecture website. Make it more modern to showcase the firms modern design style.
I would redesign the home page, portfolio pages and project pages, 3D videos, about us and the contact page.
Where we were
The homepage was chaotic in the sense the images scrolled quickly and the text was difficult to read. The fonts did not match and there was an out of place "Play" button hidden in the center. Some images were low resolution and there were far too many images on the homepage.
How we got there.
I gathered imput from other Zeterre team members of what they were hoping to see from the new site. I did a competitor analysis with other firms and pulled elements that seem to draw my attention in style, fonts and simplicity.
The redesign.
After a few rounds of iterations, the site redesign was finalized and Zeterre Landscape Architecture is now updated to a modern design that is well above it's top competitors.
